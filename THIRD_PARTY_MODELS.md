# Third-Party LTspice Models

This repository contains external SPICE models and LTspice symbols used by the simulation, including models for:

- 74HCT logic devices
- CD4000-series devices
- CD4050B buffer
- CD4066 analog switch
- LM311 comparator
- TL07x operational amplifier

These model and symbol files are included to keep the LTspice project reproducible. They are not presented as original work.

Before redistributing or relicensing the repository, verify the original source and redistribution terms of each third-party model.
